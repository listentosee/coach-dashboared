# Mayors Cyber Cup Slide 1 Prompt

## Prompt

Create a single landscape presentation slide (16:9) for high school students, teachers, school leaders, and community supporters.

This slide should feel warm, human, confident, and visually striking. Use a mature American graphic novel aesthetic with cinematic comic-book energy. The people and icons should feel more grounded, slightly gritty, and more mature, while still being hopeful and school-appropriate.

### Visual style

- mature American graphic novel aesthetic
- cinematic comic-book energy
- bold inked outlines
- dramatic lighting and shadow
- textured gradients and halftone-inspired depth
- expressive human figures with more realism and edge
- slightly gritty, grounded, and confident
- still warm, hopeful, and school-appropriate
- avoid childish cartoon styling
- avoid parody or direct use of existing comic characters or logos
- no map
- no tables
- no footer
- no admin UI
- no topic clustering
- no doughnut charts
- do NOT duplicate data for visual balance. 

### Design mood

- community pride
- student momentum
- civic energy
- strong but welcoming
- faculty and students should feel capable, determined, and inspired
