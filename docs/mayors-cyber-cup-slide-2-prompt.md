# Mayors Cyber Cup Slide 2 Prompt

## Prompt

Create a single landscape presentation slide (16:9) for high school students, teachers, school leaders, and community supporters.

This slide should feel warm, human, energetic, and visually powerful. Use a mature American graphic novel aesthetic with cinematic comic-book energy. The students should feel determined, capable, and real, with a little grit and edge, but still fully appropriate for schools, faculty, and families.

### Visual style

- mature American graphic novel aesthetic
- cinematic comic-book energy
- bold inked outlines
- dramatic lighting and shadow
- textured gradients and halftone-inspired depth
- expressive human figures with more realism and edge
- slightly gritty, grounded, and confident
- still warm, hopeful, and school-appropriate
- avoid childish cartoon styling
- avoid parody or direct use of existing comic characters or logos
- no map
- no tables
- no footer
- no admin UI
- no topic clustering
- no doughnut charts
- NEVER duplicate data sections

### Design mood

- teamwork
- resilience
- curiosity
- hands-on energy
- student confidence and belonging
